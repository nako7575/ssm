Space Smart Museum

Space Smart Museum is a blockchain virtual platform on the metaverse where people all over the world can equally enjoy communicating through art and culture. It orbits 400 km above the ground, the same distance as the International Space Station. I imagined a place where people from all over the world can gather in peace, so, created an organic structure with lots of nature. This place is an oasis floating in space.
